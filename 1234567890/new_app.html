Business Plan:

1. Key Business Elements

Main Platform Features:

  • Activity Tracking: Running, cycling, hiking, swimming, etc. Detailed analysis of routes, distances, pace, and elevation.
  • Social Network: Activity sharing, interaction (likes, comments, challenges). Groups and clubs by interests.
  • Training Analytics: User progress, reports, personalized recommendations.
  • Marketplace: Offers for sports equipment, training programs, services.
  • Gamification: Rewards, badges, challenges, leaderboards.

Monetization:

  • Subscriptions: Free basic version + premium ($5-10/month).
  • Advertising: Sports-related products and services.
  • Partnerships: Integrations with Garmin, Wahoo, Polar, etc.
  • Events: Selling access to sports events.

3. Development Stages and ETA

  • Research and Planning (1-2 months): Competitor analysis, audience research, technical requirements.
  • Prototyping (2 months): Interface design for web and mobile apps.
  • MVP Development (6 months): Core functionality (activity tracking, social network). Web and mobile apps.
  • Testing (2 months): Regression, functional, performance testing.
  • Launch (1 month): Release on Google Play and App Store.
  • Support and Scaling: Adding new features, integrations.

4. Technical Architecture

  • Programming Language: Python (Django) for backend, React/Flutter for frontend.
  • Databases: PostgreSQL/MySQL.
  • Cloud Infrastructure: AWS/GCP/Azure.
  • API: REST/GraphQL for integrations.
  • Tracking and Analytics: Mapbox, OpenStreetMap, Firebase.

5. Integration with Garmin/Wahoo

  1. Access APIs: Register the app on Garmin and Wahoo developer portals.
  2. OAuth2 Authorization: Users connect their devices to the platform.
  3. Data Synchronization: Fetch workout data, routes, metrics.
  4. Callback Functions: Real-time activity updates.

6. Marketing Strategy

  • Website Development: Promo materials (1 month).
  • Social Media: TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, Twitter (short videos).
  • Partnerships: Collaborate with trainers and athletes.
  • Advertising Campaigns: Google Ads, Facebook Ads.
